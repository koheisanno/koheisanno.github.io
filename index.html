Celestial Bodies Simulation using Javascript

25 May 2019

This is a simulation of celestial bodies and how they would interact according to the law of gravitation.

There are a few options: creating individual bodies, creating multiple randomized bodies, or creating two bodies such that one orbits around the other in a circular path.

Upon collision, the two bodies would combine and form a larger body of mass.

Modeling the Gas Laws with a Simulation

1 Sep 2019

The Kinetic Molecular Theory (KMT) is a set of postulates that help explain why gasses follow the ideal gas law: PV = nRT.

I decided to create a 2D model of an ideal gas that follows the Kinetic Molecular Theory using the pygame module in python, whereby I could specify the area of the “container”, the number of particles, as well as the average kinetic energy of the particles.
